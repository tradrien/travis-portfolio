The following is a poem that was written by Travis in January 2007:

"She Has Me"

Oh, how quickly you will see,
how she has won me, ... my heart's glee,

Her with that long black hair,
and those eyes,
into which I am drawn to stare,
yet I tell you she doesn't care

To me, she said, "I like this other guy a lot,"
and even had I fought,
I'd have nought but this measly pen
and this love-sick heart     beat

This lovely lady,
had me upon the very day we met,
and so it is,
she has me yet.
