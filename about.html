Although Travis works in Information Technology, you will find here that he also has an interest in poetry

By profession, Travis is an IT Analyst working in the higher education sector. He has been working full-time in IT Services at post secondary institutions since September 2018. While he was in the Computer Information Systems Administration diploma program, Travis worked at BCIT IT Services as a student employee. He held his student employee role in IT Services from June 2017 - May 2018.

Travis now works on a team that focuses on client hardware and software (both deployment and support). In some of his previous roles, he covered a more broad range of work within IT. In these roles, he carried out IT Service Desk, Audio Visual Services, as well as Client Hardware/Software-type work.

Travis is also a hobbyist poet/writer. He always enjoyed doing creative writing assignments in school. He also wrote a number of poems when he was a teenager and has thought about publishing a poetry book one day.

After having watched "Pay It Forward," Travis decided he would like to get into acting. He was so moved by the story and Haley Joel Osment's acting in the movie. He wanted to be able to move others like he had been.

At the age of 12, Travis started his acting journey by taking acting classes locally in the Fraser Valley. In grade 8, he got a role as a soldier in the school's performance of Romanoff & Juliet. The next year, he played the housekeeper in the Sound of Music.

He has considered pursuing community theatre in recent years. When he saw "Jesus Christ Superstar" performed by a local theatre group, it reminded him how much he enjoyed being part of the theatre.
